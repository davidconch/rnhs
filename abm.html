ABM (Accountancy, Business, and Management)

ABM stands for Accountancy, Business, and Management. It is an academic strand offered in senior high school in the Philippines that is designed to prepare students for careers in the fields of accounting, business, entrepreneurship, and management.

The ABM strand is ideal for students who are interested in pursuing a career in business or accounting. It provides a strong foundation in business and accounting principles, as well as in-depth knowledge and skills in financial management, marketing, economics, and entrepreneurship.

Students who choose the ABM strand will take subjects such as Business Ethics and Social Responsibility, Principles of Marketing, Fundamentals of Accounting, Business Finance, Organization and Management, and Business Math. They will also have the opportunity to participate in work immersion programs and other activities that will allow them to gain practical experience and develop their skills in real-world settings.

Upon completion of the ABM strand, students can pursue further education in business or accounting, or enter the workforce and pursue careers in a variety of fields, such as finance, marketing, management, accounting, and entrepreneurship. The ABM strand equips students with the skills and knowledge they need to succeed in a rapidly changing and dynamic global economy.
