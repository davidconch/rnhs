HE (Home Economics)

HE stands for Home Economics, and it is a course that provides students with the skills and knowledge required to manage a household and maintain a healthy lifestyle. In the HE course, students will learn about different topics such as cooking and food preparation, garment construction, home management, family health and wellness, and child development. HE provides students with practical skills that they can apply in their personal lives and future careers in the food, hospitality, and tourism industries.
