WELCOME TO RNHS!

Welcome to Rosales National High School, a premier educational institution located in the heart of Rosales, Pangasinan. Our school is committed to providing quality education to students from all walks of life, with a focus on academic excellence, holistic development, and community service.

Established in 1973, Rosales National High School has a rich history of academic and extracurricular achievements. Our faculty and staff are highly qualified and dedicated to helping our students reach their full potential. We offer a comprehensive curriculum that includes subjects in the arts, sciences, humanities, and technology, as well as a wide range of sports, clubs, and organizations that cater to the diverse interests of our students.

At Rosales National High School, we believe in developing not only the intellectual capacity of our students, but also their social, emotional, and physical well-being. We encourage our students to become responsible, compassionate, and engaged members of society, and to use their talents and skills to make a positive impact in their communities and beyond.

We invite you to explore our website and learn more about our school, our programs, and our values. Whether you're a current student, a prospective student, a parent, or a community member, we hope that you'll find the information you need and that you'll consider joining us in our mission to educate and empower the next generation of leaders.
