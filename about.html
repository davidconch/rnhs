ABOUT RNHS

Established in 1973, Rosales National High School has a rich history of academic and extracurricular achievements. Our faculty and staff are highly qualified and dedicated to helping our students reach their full potential. We offer a comprehensive curriculum that includes subjects in the arts, sciences, humanities, and technology, as well as a wide range of sports, clubs, and organizations that cater to the diverse interests of our students.
