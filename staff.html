STAFFS & FACULTIES

Principal - The principal is responsible for the overall management and administration of the school. He or she ensures that the school operates smoothly and efficiently and provides leadership and guidance to the teaching staff and students.

Assistant Principal - The assistant principal supports the principal in the management and administration of the school. He or she assists in overseeing the day-to-day operations of the school, as well as providing guidance and support to the teaching staff and students.

Guidance Counselors - The guidance counselors provide academic and personal counseling to students, helping them to identify their strengths and weaknesses and develop strategies to achieve their goals. They also provide guidance on career planning, college applications, and other issues related to student well-being and success.

Subject Teachers - The subject teachers are responsible for teaching specific subjects to students, providing them with knowledge and skills in various fields such as English, Mathematics, Science, Social Studies, and more. They also work closely with the guidance counselors to monitor student progress and provide support and feedback as needed.

Non-Teaching Staff - The non-teaching staff at Rosales National High School includes administrative personnel, clerks, and support staff who work to ensure that the school runs smoothly and efficiently. They provide assistance to students, parents, and staff members and help to maintain the school's facilities and resources.
