GAS (General Academic Strand)

GAS stands for General Academic Strand. It is an academic strand offered in senior high school in the Philippines that is designed to provide students with a broad and balanced education in various disciplines.

The GAS strand is ideal for students who are still exploring their career options or have not yet decided on a specific field they would like to pursue. It offers a range of subjects in different disciplines such as mathematics, natural sciences, social sciences, and humanities.

Students who choose the GAS strand will take subjects such as Humanities, Social Sciences, Natural Sciences, Disaster Readiness and Risk Reduction, Creative Writing, Media and Information Literacy, and Statistics and Probability. The GAS strand aims to develop students' critical thinking, problem-solving, research, and communication skills.
