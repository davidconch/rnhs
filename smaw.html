SMAW (Shielded Metal Arc Welding)

SMAW stands for Shielded Metal Arc Welding, and it is a course that provides students with the skills and knowledge required to weld metal parts together using an electric arc. In the SMAW course, students will learn about different types of welding equipment, welding safety practices, welding techniques, and welding materials. SMAW provides students with practical skills that they can apply in their future careers in industries such as construction, shipbuilding, and metal fabrication.
