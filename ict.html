ICT (Information and Communications Technology)

ICT stands for Information and Communication Technology. It is an academic strand offered in senior high school in the Philippines that is designed to provide students with the skills and knowledge required to work in the field of technology and information.

The ICT strand is ideal for students who are interested in pursuing a career in the field of technology and information. It offers a range of subjects that focus on the principles and applications of these disciplines. Students who choose the ICT strand will take subjects such as Computer Programming, Web Development, Database Management, Networking, and Digital Media Production.
