STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics)

STEM stands for Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics. It is an academic strand offered in senior high school in the Philippines that is designed to provide students with a strong foundation in science, technology, engineering, and mathematics.

The STEM strand is ideal for students who are interested in pursuing a career in the fields of science, technology, engineering, and mathematics. It offers a range of subjects that focus on the principles and applications of these disciplines.
